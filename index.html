Welcome to Vinta Resort Hotel.

Vinta Resort is a hotel situated in the surburbs around Riat Hills,Kisumu County. The resort face the hills of Riat thus giving a good and clear glance of the sun setting and an impressive view of Lake Victoria from the rare side. Possesing a large compound, the resort boasts of offering the best conferencing halls, accomodation rooms, children's play area and parks for hosting outdoor events. The resort also brags of the numerous awards it has won, both nationally and internationally through its humanitarian activities.

  • Vision: Giving the best services to our customers and the community we live in.
  • Motto: Luxurious, Deluxe, Pleasant.
